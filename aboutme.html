Hi, I'm Cassie, a junior biological sciences major with concentrations in Cellular and Developmental Biology at Carnegie Mellon University. After my cancer-related research experiences, I have become driven to obtain a Ph.D. in Cancer Biology on my way to pursuing a career in elucidating carcinogenesis mechanisms and generating targeted therapies for molecules implicated in those carcinogenesis pathways. Not only do I have a passion for creating knowledge, but I also enjoy sharing knowledge. I hope to become a researcher at a university where I can also teach and train the next generation of scientists!

At Carnegie Mellon, I am involved with a number of things including my ongoing research with the McCartney lab, being a Supplemental Instruction leader and student supervisor with the Academic Development program, and being the co-president of the Mellon College of Science Student Advisory Council. Please feel free to peruse my website to find out more about me!
