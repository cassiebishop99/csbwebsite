I am involved with a number of things here at Carnegie Mellon, including being an undergraduate researcher with the McCartney Lab, being a Supplemental Instruction leader and student supervisor with the Academic Development program, and being the co-president of the Mellon College of Science Student Advisory Council. Please feel free to peruse my website to find out more about me!
